About Us

Cosplayer, Not the Character is a multimedia project about cosplay in conventions which aims to strengthen the presence of the "Cosplay is Not Consent" campaign in the Philippines by educating convention attendees.

MISSION

Cosplayer, Not The Character raises awareness about misbehavior and sexual harassment in the cosplay community at pop culture conventions in the Philippines. It educates convention attendees about the "Cosplay is Not Consent" campaign, convention etiquette and policies, and cosplay.

VISION

This project aims to make pop culture conventions safer for cosplayers to enjoy and express themselves freely and empower cosplayers to speak up against misbehavior and sexual harassment.

OUR TEAM

Cosplayer, Not the Character is made by AB Multimedia Arts students of the De La Salle-College of Saint Benilde. This multimedia project was created solely for academic purposes.
