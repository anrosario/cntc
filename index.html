A costume is not an open invitation to a cosplayer's body. Cosplayers are real people, not the fictional characters they portray. Treat them with the same respect as other people.

Making pop culture

conventions

safer for the community

Cosplayer, Not the Character is an informative multimedia project that aims to educate convention attendees about proper convention etiquette and guidelines.

More about us

Is “Cosplay is Not Consent” enough?

The "Cosplay is Not Consent" movement aims to raise awareness on sexual harassment of cosplayers. Despite widespread awareness among cosplayers, convention attendees, and event organizers, the issue still persists in the community.
