CONVENTION ETIQUETTE

Convention Guidelines

Planning on attending a convention? Make sure that you have read about the guidelines of the convention that you are attending. We compiled the policies of local event organizers. Follow them to ensure a safe and enjoyable experience for everyone.

The guidelines below are compiled from official sources to aid convention goers. These are updated accordingly as they are changed by the organizers.

Contact us if you have any concerns regarding the guidelines listed below. Let us know as well if you want yours to be added.

Anime Alliance

Updated guidelines as of 2018

Established in 2006, Anime Alliance is one of the pioneers in providing anime and cosplay event services in the Philippines. It is an event partner of ToyCon, the longest pop culture event in the Philippines.

Read guidelines

Best of Anime

Our team is still securing a copy of the organizer's guidelines.

Best of Anime is a major Japanese pop culture convention in the Philippines established by Primetrade Asia, Inc.

Read guidelines

Cosplay.ph

Updated guidelines as of September 17, 2023

Cosplay.ph, founded in 2008, is a major Japanese pop culture organizer in the Philippines. It organizes four annual events: Cosplay Mania, Cosplay Matsuri, Cosplay Carnival, and Anime and Cosplay Expo (ACX).

Read guidelines

Electronic Sports and Gaming Summit (ESGS)

Our team is still securing a copy of the organizer's guidelines.

One of the major gaming conventions in Southeast Asia where the gaming community gathers to celebrate innovations in esports and games.

Read guidelines

Ozine Fest

Our team is still securing a copy of the organizer's guidelines.

An anime and cosplay convention that focuses on organizing events without admission fees in venues such as Ayala Malls for those who are unable to attend events at large convention venues like the SMX Convention Center.
