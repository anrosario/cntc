CONVENTION ETIQUETTE

Cosplay.ph

The guidelines for events organized by Cosplay.ph are provided in this page.

Cosplay Mania

Guidelines as of September 17, 2023

The violations of the venue security rules and regulations (such as going against authorized personnel, violating health and safety checks, having prohibited items at the venue on the day of the event, and going against the health and safety policies)

Misbehavior such as being uncooperative, unruly, and disrespectful towards fellow attendees, convention organizers, staff, and security personnel

Offenses that cause disturbance/danger (such as threats, harassment, stalking, physical force) towards fellow convention attendees

Offensive, unruly, and lewd language (such as profanity, obscene gestures, slurs) that are used to disturb others

False accusations are treated as an act of harassment in itself and will be dealt with appropriately.

Disturbing public peace, unlawful acts, and/or offensive behavior due to a group assembly

Fabrication, sharing/passing, illegal duplication of wristbands and ID badges

Solicitation or economic activity within the venue/area of the convention that is not approved by the convention organizers or does not comply with the rules of the organizers (Not limited to: selling of unauthorized products and services, unapproved peddling of products and services, soliciting donations, and distributing promotional materials/advertising)

Display signs of any kind, regardless if it is for a costume prop or not, that goes against the preceding rule (signs for selling/advertise or instigates unruly actions)

Unapproved signs are confiscated and the repetition of offenses will result in the individual being removed from the premises.

The possession of any dangerous items or can be used as a weapon (such as firearms, knives, explosives, clubs, whips or any item that can pose as danger to fellow attendees)

Alcoholic drinks under the possession and consumed by minors and adults

There are specified areas for adults to drink by the convention organizers or venue officials. The distribution of such substances to minors or is clearly intoxicated by these substances are penalized

The possession of illegal substances or drugs (such as narcotics or marijuana)

In compliance with the Comelec Total Gun Ban for 2023 BSKE, possessions of guns, firearms, and explosives regardless if it is a replica or not

Bringing foods and drinks from outside SMX

Tumblers and water bottles that are refillable are allowed.

Smoking within non-smoking areas

Violation of any law or local government rule

Proper public behavior, interactions, courtesy, and respect for private property are expected to be followed and respected.

  • Cosplay Mania 2023 staff and authorized personnel will enforce the Code of Conduct and Discipline
  • Cosplay Mania 2023 and the venue personnel has the right to refuse entry to any individual or group who are found to violate these rules. Those who are found to violate these rules will be subject to being removed from the area.
  • Please refer to the Cosplay Mania 2023 security or the venue security personnel if there are concerns over another fellow attendee that has violated the Code of Conduct. Unauthorized actions by a guest that may be on behalf of the said Code of Conduct, are also subject to disciplinary actions as well.
  • The Code of Conduct are updated and modified at any time by the Cosplay Mania 2023 and its organizers.
  • Knife (pointed, sharp, or made of metal)
  • Sword (pointed, sharp, or made of metal)
  • Spear (pointed, sharp, or made of metal)
  • Pointed scissors
  • Box Cutter
  • Real Firearms
  • Airsoft Firearms
  • Airsoft Compressed Air Cans
  • Airsoft Batteries
  • Gun Magazines
  • Photography and videography are generally allowed in common areas of the venue except on special locations (such as Special events and Meet and Greet sessions) which has their own rules on photography and videography
  • It is highly advised to ask for permission when asking for a video or photo from cosplayers. The persistence of doing so can be considered as harassment on the photographer’s/videographer’s part.
  • There are video footage and photographs of the events, attendees, and the convention through official photographers and staff members which may be used in commercial videos, marketing materials or other media by Cosplay Mania 2023. The attendance of the event has included consent of being filmed/photographed by the official Cosplay Mania 2023 photographers and staff, authorized industry, or media during Cosplay Mania 2023 without compensation. This grants rights from Cosplay Mania 2023 to reproduce, display, distribute, perform, create derivative works from any film or footage that is binded in to any media and manner all around the world and in perpetuity
  • The photography or videography of the Cosplay Mania 2023 event, staff or attendees for the intention of publication/sale to the press is forbidden unless approved by Cosplay Mania 2023 organizers.
  • Photos and footage for private viewing is allowed These rules are applied to the attendees, guests, exhibitors, staff, and press.
