CONVENTION ETIQUETTE

Anime Alliance

The guidelines for events organized by Anime Alliance are provided in this page.

ToyCon

Guidelines as of 2018
  • Proper public behavior, interactions, courtesy, and respect for private property are expected to be followed and respected.
  • Harassing and offensive behavior is not tolerated. Toycon has the right to deny your entry to the convention, with no refunds, in not complying with this rule.
  • Immediately approach security personnel or staff if someone is threatening your safety or is aware of someone breaking code of conduct.
Handcarts, Trolleys, Rolling Luggage, or Oversized Strollers

This is for safety reasons, especially for the expected number of attendees. These are restricted to only certain areas given by the Toycon staff.
Paging

This is only for emergency uses only and restricted. Coordinate properly with your friends or family and have a proper method of communication if separated.
Pets

Includes and is not limited to parrots, iguanas, or other non-human critters. Only service animals are allowed in the venue.
Smoking (E-cigarettes & vaping products)

Smoking within the event is not allowed. Only smoke in designated areas provided by the venue area.
Airspace Restriction

Tying or flying helium balloons or any products filled with helium, drones, or anything lighter than air objects are not permitted.
Photography

Ask before shooting, regardless if you are an amateur or a professional (including the press and media). Attendees or cosplayers may not be comfortable in having their photo taken so please ask.

Photos without permission of the subject may be considered as harassment and a violation of privacy which will have certain measures taken.
Costume & Weapons Policy

For cosplay contest participants, it is required to submit/check-in their props at the registration desk to validate. Props that are considered to be dangerous will be deposited and only used on-stage with restrictions.

Anime Alliance has the right to confiscate non-validated props that are considered as dangerous for the general public.
Sitting in Exhibit Hall Aisles

For potential fire hazards, sitting at the Exhibit Hall is prohibited.
No Drugs, Alcohol and Illegal Controlled Substances

For any one caught consuming/has these items with them, Toycon shall take the steps in the following:

  • Police and security will be contacted based on the city laws and procedures, entry to the venue will be revoked with no refund, and removed from the premises and banned on future Toycon (and Anime Alliance events).
  • People found consuming alcohol will be asked to leave the venue immediately. A person heavily intoxicated and under the influence that causes a negative impact shall be removed from the venue, entrance fee with no refunds shall be revoked, and banned from future Toycon and Anime Alliance events.
No Dangerous Weapons

List of prohibited items:

  • Bladed, edged weapons, and/or live steel (swords, axes, knives, martial arts equipment, etc)
  • Projectile weapons, including plastic, are not allowed
  • Liquid dispensing weapons. In using hairspray or aerosol, it is instructed to do so outside as some of the attendees or other people within the venue may have an allergic reaction
  • Incendiaries or anything that can produce a flame, explosion or spark
  • Long props cannot exceed 2.1 meters/7 feet. Any longer may cause potential accidents and is prohibited. For certain situations, props that are around 6.5-7 feet may be asked to be deposited in a props counter and will be given during the competition (for contest participants).
  • Chains are only limited to a 1 meter in length as its maximum and chains that are potentially used as a weapon are not allowed
  • Live blades and firearms are not allowed in the venue and competitions
  • Items that are considered dangerous/harmful will be confiscated and will be returned by the end of the day

  • A CODE ADAM Alert may be broadcasted and there will be a lock-down order issued by the marshals and security for the authentic report of a missing child/children. Once this has been executed, no child is allowed to leave the venue without confirmation of their relations from their guardian/parent. When executed, Anime Alliance expects cooperation and understanding from all of the attendees for everyone’s safety.
